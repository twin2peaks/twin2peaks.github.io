Elastomeric Bridge Bearings according to BS EN 1337-3

Bearing Type

The tool supports the static design of elastomeric bridge bearings according to BS EN 1337-3 for applications in bridge and civil engineering. Reinforced elastomeric bearings are preliminarily designed taking into account the bearing loads, shear deformation and rotation capacity.

The calculation is based on the regulations of BS EN 1337, the requirements for load determination of EN 1990, as well as the recommendations of relevant standards and guidelines.
